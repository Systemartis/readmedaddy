# Figure 2 — flux by tide phase

Reproduced by `analysis.ipynb`. Mean microplastic particle count per litre,
grouped by tide phase, over the 28-day sampling window.

| Tide phase | Mean particles/L |
|---|---|
| flood | 4.25 |
| slack-high | 3.55 |
| ebb | 8.50 |
| slack-low | 3.85 |

**Headline:** ebb-tide flux is ~2.3x slack-water flux. This is the paper's
central result; the figure is regenerated from `data/samples.csv` on every run,
so the number in the abstract and the number in the notebook cannot drift apart.
